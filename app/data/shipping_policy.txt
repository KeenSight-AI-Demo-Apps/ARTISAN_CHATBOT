# Shipping Information for Artisan Products

## Overview
We offer  shipping for all our artisan products. Our goal is to deliver your handcrafted items safely and efficiently, with clear tracking and reliable service.

## Shipping Options and Costs
- **Standard Shipping**:
  - **Cost**: Free for orders over $50 USD. For orders under $50, a flat rate of $5.99 applies.
  - **Delivery Time**: 
    - United States: 5-7 business days
    - Canada: 7-10 business days
    - Australia: 10-14 business days
    - Europe: 7-12 business days
    - Asia: 10-15 business days
    - Ethiopia: 10-15 business days
    
    
- **Expedited Shipping**:
  - **Cost**: $15.99 flat rate, regardless of order size.
  - **Delivery Time**:
    - United States: 2-3 business days
    - Canada: 3-5 business days
    - Australia: 5-7 business days
    - Europe: 3-6 business days
    - Asia: 5-8 business days
    - Ethiopia: 10-15 business days
    
- **Local Pickup**: Available for customers in New York, NY, at no cost. Contact support@artisanproducts.com to arrange.

## Tracking
- All orders include tracking. Once your order ships, you’ll receive a tracking number via email. Track your package on our website or through the carrier’s portal (e.g., USPS, DHL, or local postal services).

## Shipping Restrictions
- We ship to most countries, but restrictions may apply to certain regions due to customs regulations or carrier limitations. Contact support@artisanproducts.com if your country isn’t listed at checkout.
- Some artisan products (e.g., fragile ceramics or perishable goods) may have specific shipping requirements or restrictions. These are noted on the product page.

## Customs and Duties
- International orders may incur customs fees, duties, or taxes, which are the responsibility of the recipient. Check with your local customs office for details.
- We provide all necessary documentation to facilitate smooth customs processing.

## Processing Time
- Orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Custom or made-to-order items may take 3-5 business days to process. You’ll be notified if additional time is needed.

## Returns and Damages
- If your order arrives damaged, contact support@artisanproducts.com within 7 days for a replacement or refund.
- Returns are accepted within 30 days for eligible items. See our Returns Policy for details.

## Contact
For shipping inquiries, email support@artisanproducts.com or call +1-800-555-1234. Our team is available Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM EST.
